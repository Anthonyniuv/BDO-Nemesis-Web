    Invite a Friend [Event]

    No release date

     

    Great adventurers,

     

    Welcome to the highly acclaimed Nemesis Desert Online server, a project that was created in harmony and with great love for DownSpoT and Nemesis Studios. The event that you will attend, if willingly, will be: Invite a friend to Nemesis Desert ONLINE and be rewarded for it.

     

    The event period: No start date - No end date

     

    Ask your friend to create an account and a character and you should take a screenshot in the game next to each other.

     

    You need at least it is at level 30. Your friend doesn't need it at any level, just need it in the photo. You will send the photo through Discord by clicking on the link beside. Discord .

    The player who is not at level 30 (Who called a person), will not be able to receive the valuable rewards of the event.

     

    How do I participate?

     

    You need to play on the server for a while and you are already at level 30 or higher.

    You, after inviting your friend, need to go to his location (inside the game) and take a picture next to him (showing family name and nickname).

    Both need to appear in the photo, making any gesture, or animation.

     

    I did everything properly. Where's my reward?

     

    There are two ways to achieve this. One talking to a GM for the discord, another, opening a ticket for the discord and waiting to be answered.

    “ Talking to a GM ” this is difficult, as they are always busy, so any unanswered messages in the private, we feel sorry for that.

    • Tickets are answered by interns. We do not guarantee a quick and instant response.

    • It is extremely necessary that you have a discord, to stay on top of some meetings that take place there.

     

    “ What is Discord? ” Discord is an online program that connects two or more people in a conversation, whether by voice, chat, video and / or live.

    • Everyone is participating in the event, but it's up to you whether you want to receive the rewards or not.

    • The player who invites your friend is required, is at level 30.

    • The event is a benefit for players, STAFFS will not be able to participate.

    • You have a limit of 5 invited players. We will not give more than five rewards to the player.
